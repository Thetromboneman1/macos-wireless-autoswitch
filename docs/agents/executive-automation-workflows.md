# Executive Automation Workflows

Date: 2026-06-23
Owner: Executive Communications

## Purpose

Executive automation operationalizes the Executive Communications Copilot for quarterly reviews, monthly updates, Red Hat Summit briefings, architecture updates, and automation ROI reports.

## Input Contract

| Input | Required for |
|---|---|
| Audience and decision needed | Every executive artifact |
| Source evidence and dates | Every executive artifact |
| KPI or value metric | Reviews, updates, ROI reports |
| Risks and mitigations | Reviews, architecture updates, Summit briefings |
| Owner actions | Every executive artifact |

## Workflows

| Workflow | Source agents | Output | Quality bar |
|---|---|---|---|
| Quarterly review | Operational Review, Enterprise Architecture, AAP, Satellite | Quarterly business and platform review | Decision-ready, metric-backed, risk-aware |
| Monthly update | Operational Review, Automation Discovery | Monthly status update | Clear progress, exceptions, next actions |
| Red Hat Summit briefing | Red Hat Summit Assistant, Enterprise Architecture | Executive implication brief | Announcements translated into architecture and platform impact |
| Architecture update | Enterprise Architecture | Architecture decision update | Business context, options, recommendation, approval path |
| Automation ROI report | Automation Discovery, AAP | ROI and opportunity report | Time saved, effort, next investment ask |

## Validation

An executive artifact is ready when it is concise, evidence-backed, decision-oriented, free of secret values, and scored at least 24 of 30 by the evaluation framework with an executive quality score of 5.
